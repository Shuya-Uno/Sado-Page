Sado

Calm and Warm Tea Ceremony

Sado is one of the Japanese traditional culture, that the hosts and guests enjoy a calm and heart-warming moment through serving and having of tea.

The host entertains the guest not only by the serving of tea and sweets, but providing a atmosphere that nicely represents the season or a particular theme by arranging and displaying many Japanese-style tea-sets.

By guests and the host having delightful conversations upon tea, they both cherish their meeting and the bliss of having the same time with each other.
